Frequently Asked Questions


Will game "XYZ" work?

The emulator is still a work in progress and not all games have been tested, but you can check our Github Compatiblity List, and if you spot a game that hasn't been tested or works better in newer builds you can report it there.

Is there an Android version?

Yes you can, and its been dubbed "Pandroid" (Panda + Android), however only the stable builds release for it and the compatiblity may vary from what you can get on PC or Mac.

What license does Panda3DS have?

The emulator itself is under the GPL-3.0 License, and the website is under the MIT License.

Why do Miis have a photo of a panda on them?

The miis have a photo of a panda on them because, A: the emulator is themed around pandas, and B: its a placeholder until dumping files for miis from your 3DS and using them are implemented.

How can I make the emulator portable?

To make the emulator portable, you need to open the config.toml with a text editor (like Notepad or Vim) and change "UsePortableBuild = false" to "true" and then everything the emulator needs will be in that folder Panda3DS is in.
